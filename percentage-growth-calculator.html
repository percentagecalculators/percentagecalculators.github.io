How to Calculate Business Growth Rate

Growth rate measures how much a value has increased relative to its starting point. This tool from Percentage Calculators is designed for tracking business metrics.

Growth Rate Formula

Growth Rate = ((Final Value - Initial Value) ÷ Initial Value) × 100

Business Applications

  • Revenue and sales growth
  • Customer growth
  • Investment returns
  • Website traffic growth
  • Employee growth

Example: Revenue Growth

Revenue from $500K to $650K: (($650K - $500K) ÷ $500K) × 100 = 30% growth. Need to convert data to percentages first? Use our fraction to percentage converter. To find original values from growth percentages, try our reverse percentage calculator.
