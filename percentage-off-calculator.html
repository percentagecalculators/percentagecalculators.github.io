How to Calculate Sale Price After Discount

This discount calculator from Percentage Calculators is your shopping companion. See both the sale price and savings amount instantly.

Discount Formula

Sale Price = Original Price × (1 - Discount% ÷ 100)

Stacking Discounts

When stores offer '20% off + extra 10%,' they apply sequentially: $100 → $80 (20% off) → $72 (extra 10%). Total discount is 28%, not 30%.

For comparing prices without a clear original, use our percentage difference calculator. To convert a fraction like '1/3 off' to a percentage, try our fraction to percentage converter.
