How to Find Percentage Change Between Two Numbers

Percentage change tells you how much a value has increased or decreased relative to its original amount. This percentage calculator tool instantly shows both the direction and magnitude of change.

Percentage Change Formula

Percentage Change = ((New Value - Old Value) ÷ Old Value) × 100

A positive result means increase; negative means decrease.

Example Calculation

From 50 to 65: ((65 - 50) ÷ 50) × 100 = 30% increase. From 100 to 80: ((80 - 100) ÷ 100) × 100 = -20% (20% decrease).

If you already know the percentage and want to apply a decrease, use our percentage decrease calculator. For comparing two values without a before/after relationship, try our percentage difference calculator.
