What is a Percentage?

A percentage is a way of expressing a number as a fraction of 100. The word 'percent' comes from the Latin 'per centum,' meaning 'by the hundred.' When we say 25%, we mean 25 out of every 100, or simply 25/100, which equals 0.25 as a decimal. Need to convert a fraction like 3/4 to a percentage? We have a dedicated tool for that conversion.

The Percentage Formula Explained

The basic percentage formula that powers our calculator is:

Percentage Value = (Percentage ÷ 100) × Base Number

For example, to find 25% of 200: Convert the percentage to a decimal (25 ÷ 100 = 0.25), then multiply by the base number (0.25 × 200 = 50). Therefore, 25% of 200 equals 50. Our calculator performs this instantly.
