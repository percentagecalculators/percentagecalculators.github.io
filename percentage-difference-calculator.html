How to Compare Two Numbers with Percentage Difference

Percentage difference measures how far apart two values are relative to their average. Unlike percentage change, both values are treated equally – the result is the same regardless of order. Part of the Percentage Calculators suite.

Percentage Difference Formula

Percentage Difference = (|V₁ - V₂| ÷ Average) × 100

Where Average = (V₁ + V₂) ÷ 2

When to Use This Calculator

  • Comparing prices at different stores
  • Comparing measurements from different sources
  • When neither value is the 'original'
  • When you need a symmetric comparison
